Target Audience & Medical Problem

SenseEase addresses the 97.6% of display users experiencing visual discomfort. VIMS is triggered by a sensory conflict — the visual cortex detects motion while the vestibular system registers stillness, producing nausea. DES stems from unmanaged luminance transitions between high-contrast windows.

Four-Layer Engineering Architecture
Layer 1

Sensing Layer

HP Look to Move gaze vectors, mouse/keyboard/touchpad events, and DOM scroll velocity quantify Optical Flow Intensity — zero camera, zero privacy risk.

Layer 2

Inference Layer

ALSTM-FCN rolling VIMS Pressure Index. Efference Copy weight: 0.2× Driver Mode (user-initiated) vs 1.5× Passenger Mode (passive).

Layer 3

Rendering Layer

WebGL DitheringShader pixel-level FOV mask. Chang-Hain Stationary Anchor: white grid locked to screen coordinates resists destabilizing optical flow. CSS fallback guaranteed.

Layer 4

Feedback Layer

HP IQ Chatbot collects real-time complaints. Habituation Scheduler relaxes protection at 1%/sec after 120s of silence — demonstrating adaptive brain plasticity.
